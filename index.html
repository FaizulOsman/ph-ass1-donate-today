Flood Relief Appeal For Bangladesh

Torrential rains and an onrush from the upstream have worsened flooding in parts of Northeastern India and Bangladesh. In Sylhet the flood leaving around 50,000 homes without power and forcing the authorities to open 200 shelters across the northeastern district. BD Foundation mobilizes volunteers to reach out to very remote areas with the flood relief. Thousands are stranded and need immediate food and clean water.

More information about BD Foundation, Inc.: BD Foundation is a Northern Virginia-based non-profit organization dedicated to poverty alleviation, health, sanitation, and educational projects.
